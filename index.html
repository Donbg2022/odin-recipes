This sandwich is brought up a level by toasting the bread until crispy

This recipe is very simple to make and only takes about 5 minutes of time. The sandwich is comprised of choice of meat, cheese, and vegetables. toasted bread (sourdough is my favourite) and a generous amount of mayonaise.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Vegetables
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Love

Steps

  1. Put bread in the toaster
  2. Stack meat, cheese, and vegetables on a plate
  3. Remove toast and apply a generous amount of mayo to the top and bottom
  4. Put item stack on toast and cut in diagonal
  5. enjoy
